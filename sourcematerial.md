## About
Celebrity Carpet is a premier carpet and floor covering installation service established in 1995. We have decades of experience specializing in stair work, area rugs, hand sewn details, hand binding, axminsters, border work, insert work, wiltons, and more. William Gerlich, owner and founder of Celebrity Carpet, has built a reputation around quality workmanship, reliable service, and dependability. We pride ourselves on exquisite craftsmanship, paying close attention to every detail from start to finish. Over the years, we have serviced many well-known clients working with top interior designers and carpet showrooms. We treat all our customers like celebrities, giving them expert service and quality installation where every edge is straight and every corner is perfect.
